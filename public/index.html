JDM Cars

GS-300


This is the Lexus GS 300, very popular for the Engine type used in this family sedan.
The engine comes from a very well known car in the JDM world, the 2jz.
The most common version of the 2j used is the 2jz Ge, or the none turbo version.
Car enthusiests love to engine swap these beautiful cars with the much more sought out 2jz-Gte for more POWA!

Supra Mk4


This is the Supra Mk4, this specific one is known as Paul Walkers from the movie,
Fast & Furious. This car can make a ridiculous amount of POWA.
This particular one seen in the image, made 320 HP and 315 lb-ft of torque. Although, that isn't anything compared to the underground street competitors clocking in
